Certifications & Compliance - ISO 9001, AS9100, ITAR Registered

IDFS maintains comprehensive certifications and compliance requirements for government contracting, aerospace manufacturing, and defense industry work in Fayetteville, Georgia.

Quality Management Certifications

ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System

IDFS is certified to ISO 9001:2015, demonstrating our commitment to quality management and continuous improvement. This certification ensures our manufacturing processes meet international quality standards and provide consistent, reliable results.

  • Quality management system implementation
  • Continuous improvement processes
  • Customer satisfaction focus
  • Risk-based thinking approach
  • Process approach to quality
  • Regular internal and external audits

AS9100D Aerospace Quality Management

AS9100D certification demonstrates our ability to meet stringent quality requirements of the aerospace industry. This certification is essential for manufacturing components for commercial and military aircraft, ensuring the highest levels of quality and safety.

  • Aerospace industry quality standards
  • Risk management and mitigation
  • Configuration management
  • First article inspection
  • Statistical process control
  • Supply chain management

ISO 14001 Environmental Management

ISO 14001 certification demonstrates our commitment to environmental responsibility and sustainable manufacturing practices. We implement environmental management systems to minimize environmental impact while maintaining high-quality manufacturing standards.

  • Environmental impact assessment
  • Waste reduction and recycling
  • Energy efficiency programs
  • Environmental compliance
  • Sustainable manufacturing practices
  • Environmental performance monitoring

Security & Compliance Certifications

ITAR Registration - Defense Trade Controls

IDFS is registered with the U.S. Department of State under the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR). This registration allows us to handle defense-related projects and export-controlled items, making us a trusted partner for defense contractors and government agencies.

  • Defense trade compliance
  • Export control procedures
  • Security clearance protocols
  • Controlled technical data handling
  • Defense contractor requirements
  • Government security standards

CMMC Level 2 Cybersecurity Maturity

Our CMMC Level 2 certification demonstrates our cybersecurity maturity and ability to protect controlled unclassified information (CUI). This certification is essential for working with Department of Defense contractors and handling sensitive government information.

  • Cybersecurity framework implementation
  • Controlled unclassified information protection
  • Incident response procedures
  • Access control and authentication
  • Data encryption and protection
  • Security awareness training

NIST 800-171 Compliance

IDFS complies with NIST 800-171 requirements for protecting controlled unclassified information in non-federal systems and organizations. This compliance ensures that we meet the cybersecurity requirements for government contractors and defense industry work.

  • Information system security
  • Access control implementation
  • Data protection measures
  • Security monitoring and logging
  • Incident response capabilities
  • Regular security assessments
