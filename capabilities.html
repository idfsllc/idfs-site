Manufacturing Capabilities & Facilities - Precision CNC Machining & Welding Services

Our Fayetteville, Georgia machine shop offers comprehensive manufacturing capabilities including CNC machining, welding, fabrication, and contract manufacturing services.

CNC Machining Services

Precision CNC Milling & Turning

Multi-axis machining centers with advanced tooling for complex geometries and tight tolerances. We operate Haas VF-2 and VF-4 vertical machining centers, Okuma LB3000 lathe with live tooling, and Mazak Integrex multi-tasking machines.

Precision machining services achieve ±0.0005" tolerances with aluminum, steel, titanium, and exotic alloys for aerospace, defense, automotive, and industrial clients throughout Georgia.

  • Multi-axis CNC milling centers
  • CNC turning with live tooling
  • ±0.0005" tolerance capabilities
  • Aluminum, steel, titanium machining
  • Complex geometry manufacturing
  • Prototype and production runs

Welding & Fabrication Services

Professional Welding Services

Certified welders provide TIG, MIG, and Stick welding services for aluminum, stainless steel, carbon steel, and specialty alloys in aerospace, defense, and industrial applications.

Welding services comply with ASME and AWS standards with full documentation and traceability. Custom jigs and fixtures ensure consistent, high-quality welds for complex assemblies.

  • TIG, MIG, and Stick welding
  • Aluminum and stainless steel welding
  • ASME and AWS standards compliance
  • Custom jigs and fixtures
  • Welding documentation and traceability
  • Quality inspection and testing

Surface Treatments & Finishing

Comprehensive Coating Services

Surface treatment and finishing services enhance durability and appearance of manufactured components. Coating capabilities include anodizing, powder coating, painting, passivation, and plating services.

Surface treatment services meet aerospace, defense, and industrial specifications. We work with customers to select appropriate coating systems for specific applications and environmental requirements.

  • Anodizing (Type II and Type III)
  • Powder coating and painting
  • Passivation and chemical treatments
  • Plating services
  • Surface preparation and cleaning
  • Quality inspection and testing

Contract Manufacturing Services

Complete Manufacturing Solutions

Contract manufacturing services provide complete solutions from prototype to production. We offer rapid prototyping, mechanical assembly, quality inspection, documentation, packaging, and shipping services.

We work closely with customers to optimize designs for manufacturability, reduce costs, and improve quality. Our ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management system ensures consistent, reliable manufacturing processes.

  • Rapid prototyping and 3D printing
  • Mechanical assembly and testing
  • Quality inspection and documentation
  • Packaging and shipping services
  • Design for manufacturability
  • Supply chain management
