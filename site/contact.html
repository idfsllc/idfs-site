Maximum file size: 10MB per file. Supported formats: PDF, DXF, STEP

What to Include in Your Quote Request

  • Part Drawings: Detailed engineering drawings with dimensions and tolerances
  • Material Specifications: Type of material, grade, and any special requirements
  • Quantities: Prototype quantities, production volumes, and delivery schedule
  • Quality Requirements: Inspection requirements and documentation needs
  • Timeline: Required delivery dates and any critical milestones
  • Special Requirements: Packaging or other specifications

Our Quote Process

  1. Submit RFQ with project details and files
  2. Engineering team reviews requirements
  3. Detailed quote provided within 24 hours
  4. Schedule meeting to discuss project
  5. Begin manufacturing upon approval
