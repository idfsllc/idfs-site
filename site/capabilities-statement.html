COMPANY OVERVIEW

IDFS specializes in high-precision metal fabrication and machining, with a focus on ground support equipment for aircraft maintenance, including stands, lifts, and custom carts for handling wings and other large components. Our services include CNC milling, turning, welding, and custom prototypes. We are registered under NAICS codes 332710, 332311, and 332999, demonstrating our ability to serve industrial, aerospace, and defense-related projects.
